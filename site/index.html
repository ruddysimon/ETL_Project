ETL Project

Group Members: Victor Galstyan, Ruddy Simonpour, Patrick Hennessey

Idea

For our ETL Project our goal was to identify salary trends across various professional sports. We decided to compare salaries within the sports themselves and against each other. The sports that we decided to go with are NBA, NFL, MLB, and MLS.

We collected historical data on each sport to analyze a 10 year trend in the sports salary progression and then we used this dataset to analyze the individual sports YoY growth and how each sports compares to identify the highest paying professional sport.
